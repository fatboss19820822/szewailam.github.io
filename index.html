About Us

Hinoak is a vibrant Korean barbecue restaurant nestled in the heart of Melbourne. With its authentic flavors and inviting ambiance, it offers a delightful culinary experience. From sizzling grilled meats to mouthwatering side dishes, Hinoak aims to transport diners to the rich and flavorful world of Korean cuisine.

The menu goes beyond barbecue, offering an array of traditional Korean dishes bursting with freshness and complexity. From crispy and savory pancakes to hearty stews and vibrant vegetable sides, Hinoak presents a culinary journey through the diverse flavors of Korea. With its dedication to quality and passion for Korean cuisine, Hinoak stands as a true destination for food lovers.

As guests enter, they are greeted by the tantalizing aromas wafting from sizzling grills and the warm smiles of the attentive staff. Hinoak's menu showcases an impressive selection of premium meats, from tender marinated beef to succulent pork belly, ensuring a delightful barbecue experience. Complementing the grilled delights are an array of banchan, the vibrant and flavorful assortment of Korean side dishes, which add depth and variety to the meal.
